What is SHAP?
SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) tells us how much each feature contributed to a specific prediction.

🔴 High positive SHAP → Feature pushed prediction toward FRAUD
🟢 High negative SHAP → Feature pushed prediction toward LEGITIMATE

This makes our model interpretable — crucial for banking compliance and regulatory audits.
